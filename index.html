About Us

Nestled in the heart of Netrokona, Nabab Bari Eco Park is a sanctuary where nature and adventure come together. Our park offers a unique blend of lush greenery, thrilling rides, and serene spots for relaxation. Whether you're looking to unwind in our beautifully maintained gardens, enjoy a family picnic, or experience the excitement of our amusement rides, Nabab Bari Eco Park has something for everyone. Our mission is to provide a memorable experience that connects you with nature while offering modern amenities for your comfort and enjoyment.
